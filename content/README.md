# Tray 6 · Content & language system

> The most neglected, highest-leverage tray. AI surfaces live or die on their words. This tray is the **source of truth for copy** — components quote it, they don't hardcode it.

Pages:

- [Voice & tone](./voice-and-tone.md) — how the assistant sounds
- [Disclosure copy](./disclosure-copy.md) — what we say about being AI
- [Error & refusal microcopy](./error-and-refusal-microcopy.md) — failure and "no"
- [Prompt scaffolding](./prompt-scaffolding.md) — starter/empty-state prompts

## Principle

Treat copy as a token. A refusal message is defined once here and referenced by every surface, the same way `action.primary` is defined once and referenced by every button. When the wording changes, it changes in one place.
